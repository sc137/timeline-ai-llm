1967 Golden Years

ELIZA Chatbot

Joseph Weizenbaum at MIT creates ELIZA, the first natural language processing chatbot. Simulates a Rogerian psychotherapist using pattern matching. Users become emotionally attached despite its simplicity.

1974 Funding Shift

AI Funding Reduction

High expectations from the 1960s go unmet due to computational limitations. Symbolic AI approaches hit fundamental barriers. DARPA reduces funding for general AI research, shifting focus to more targeted applications.

1980 Expert Systems

Expert Systems Boom

Renewed government funding from DARPA. Focus shifts to rule-based expert systems for domain-specific applications. Industry begins investing in specialized AI hardware.
